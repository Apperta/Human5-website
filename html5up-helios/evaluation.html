Critical Evaluation


System Architechure

We were forced to change the architecture of the project during the implementation of the Proof of Concept. The reason was that the original proposed solution of the app with the additional â€˜Admin featureâ€™ was unsuccessful
After the discussion, we decided to drop this feature, and instead take up and implement Group Goals (another advanced feature). The Admin Feature, however, still remains open to implementation as a future improvement.



User Interface Design and User Experience

Some of the most important heuristics that we followed are found in our HCI section, along with research into them. Our central focus for User Experience is convenience and naturalness of interaction. Following this, we have tried to implement a simple yet elegant, and intuitive interface for the User interaction.


Reasoning Behind the App

The reason for which we chose to build this app is that most people nowadays are moving towards a mobile platform. Even though a website is responsive, it does not offer the same User experience as an app, for the app offers more wider selection of the User Preferences and Quick Searching.


Functionality

We have implemented all our functional and non-functional requirements fully, with the exception of the Admin User, and Goal Deadline Notification


Stability

The Human Five operates under various environments without crashing.


Compatibility

TThe app compatibility was tested across various devices and popular environments. To test the app compatibility, we made use of various simulators and emulators to assess the app. The team also used â€˜Ionic Viewâ€™ to run the app on various devices like IPhone 6S, Iphone 6S Plus, Iphone 5, Google Pixel, Samsung S7 etc.


Testability

The app has been tested on multiple devices along with Unit and Integration Testing. Furthermore, it has been manually tested such that most features proved to be working without issues (except for notifications). Alongside this, User Acceptance was carried out to get a better grasp of the App design, functionality and User Experience.


Install-ability

It is designed for iOS and Android devices and will soon be available on the corresponding mobile markets i.e. iOS Application Store and Google Play Store.


Future Works


With six more months to work with this project, we would have worked upon:

  • Designing an interaction system between people sharing goals within groups.
  • Sharing data for remote access by 3rd party
  • Implementing feature supporting the creation of an admin user (to certain other users), who can view the reports and statistics of other users and give feedback on the same.
  • Implementing notifications for informing the user about upcoming goal deadlines, new feedback from selected admin and new messages on groups
